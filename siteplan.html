Website Plan

Site Name

Name: EcoTravel Explorer

Reason for Selection: The name "EcoTravel Explorer" was selected to reflect the website's focus on sustainable and eco-friendly travel options.

Site Purpose

The purpose of "EcoTravel Explorer" is to provide information and resources for travelers who are interested in sustainable tourism. The site will offer tips on eco-friendly travel practices, highlight destinations that prioritize sustainability, and feature travel guides and articles on green travel.

Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: A visitor is looking for eco-friendly travel destinations for their next vacation.
  • Scenario 2: A visitor wants to learn about sustainable travel practices and how they can reduce their carbon footprint while traveling.

Typography
